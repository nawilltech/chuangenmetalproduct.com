About Us.

Chuangen Metal Products is located in Czech Republic. Our company is specialized in stainless steel export management, such as 201, 202, 304, 304 l, 321, 316, 316 l, 904 l, 316 ti, 310s material .More than 60% of the products are sold to Southeast Asia, Middle East and Europe, and all products are trusted by customers at home and abroad.We always adhere to the principle of quality first.Sustainable cooperation is our permanent pursuit.

Stainless steel plate: 202 cr16mn8ni5n (1) 304 (0 cr18ni9) 304 l (00 cr19ni10), 321 (1 cr18ni9ti) 316 (0 cr17ni12mo2) 316 l (00 cr17ni14mo2) 310 s (316 t (0, 0 cr25ni20) cr18ni12mo2ti 904 (00 cr20ni25mo4)

Company factory: drawing the paster, 6 k, 8 k mirror, polishing, yellow titanium, titanium black, rose gold, no fingerprints, etc. Various color stainless steel plate.

Fold to processing: roof gutter, cyclotomic, cut, and other processing. Specifications: high temperature resistance, corrosion resistance, resistance to sulfuric acid. The company's business philosophy: "the same products than quality, same quality than price, the same price, the same service than the credibility of service"

Service tenet: the abundant strength, the high quality products, low prices. Commitment: to ensure a good product, excellent quality, low price, perfect service to thank new and old customers' trust. Management principle: customers first, good quality, variety complete, the price is reasonable.

Packing Service

Professional and up-to-date packaging methods are a standard for Our forwarder. Here they support you, of course, also in the selection of the best packaging for your stainless steel and metal products. Your goods should arrive safely and in perfect condition.

Decoiling Service

Decoiling is the process of unwinding and cutting to length the steel coil. ... This results in a perfectly flat sheet of steel that is suitable for'lights out' manufacturing. Our extensive experience of the process ensures that the customers' requirements are met , every time.

Slitting Service

Coil slitting is a process involving equipment with rotary knives with which a full-width coil is cut into the customer engineered widths and required coil size and weight.

Cutting Service

We are able to custom cut our range of steel products to size through our premium cutting services: Plasma cutting/ CNC laser cutting /Band saw cutting

Polishing Service

Metal polishing smooths the surfaces of fabricated metal pieces such as sheets, angles, and pipes, often providing the final finish before parts or materials go to their end use. Polishing stainless steel is highly beneficial, as it noticeably improves its resistance to damage caused by corrosion, environmental factors, wear, and impacts.
